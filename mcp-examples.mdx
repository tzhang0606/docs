---
title: "Examples"
description: "Example prompts demonstrating core Teli MCP Server functionality."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Create an SMS agent and launch a campaign">
    **Prompt:** "Create an SMS agent for following up with mortgage leads, then launch a campaign to my contacts."

    1. Claude helps you craft a prompt and starting message
    2. Creates the SMS agent with your configuration
    3. Maps your contacts to Teli fields and launches the campaign
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Set up a voice agent and run a bulk calling campaign">
    **Prompt:** "Set up a voice agent using a female voice and start an outbound campaign to my 500 leads."

    1. Lists available voices for you to choose from
    2. Creates the voice agent with your prompt and settings
    3. Shows your available phone numbers and launches the campaign
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Add contacts to an existing campaign">
    **Prompt:** "Add these 10 new contacts to my November outreach campaign."

    1. Identifies your campaign by name
    2. Maps contact fields and confirms the mapping with you
    3. Adds the leads and reports how many were successfully added
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Update a voice agent's prompt and voicemail">
    **Prompt:** "Update my outbound sales agent — change the prompt to focus on refinance leads and add a voicemail message."

    1. Finds the agent and fetches its current configuration
    2. Presents the proposed changes for your confirmation
    3. Applies the updated prompt and voicemail message
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Check campaign progress">
    **Prompt:** "How many calls have been made in my current voice campaign and what's the completion percentage?"

    1. Lists your voice campaigns
    2. Returns progress: calls made, pending, and completion percentage
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
