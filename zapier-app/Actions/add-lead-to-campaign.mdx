---
title: "Zapier Action - Add Lead to Campaign"
description: "Here's a step-by-step guide on how to find, connect and activate the Add Lead to Campaign Zapier action. This action allows you to add leads to an active campaign."
---

## Step 1

Click on the **"Action"** step in the Zapier workflow and type in **"Teli AI"** in the search bar.

![Screenshot 2025-11-11 161813.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-11161813.png)

## Step 2

Click **"Account"** and sign in to your Teli AI account.

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 115814.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12115814.PNG)

## Step 3

Click the **"Trigger event"** menu options and select **"Add Lead to Campaign"**.

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 115955.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12115955.PNG)

## Step 4

Click on the **"Configure"** tab. Click on the **"Campaign"** menu options. This displays all your available SMS campaigns. Select the campaign you'd like to add leads to. Minimum requirements for this action is the campaign and phone number.

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 120156.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12120156.PNG)

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 120937.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12120937.PNG)

## Step 5

Once you've finished building yor zap and published it, whenever leads pass through the integration they should populate in the campaign you've selected.

(Note: It's highly advised that you start with a test lead to ensure the lead is populating in the correct campaign.)

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 121633.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12121633.PNG)
