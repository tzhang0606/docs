---
title: "Zapier Action - Upload Lead to Lead Group"
description: "Here's a step-by-step guide on how to find, connect and activate the Upload Lead to Lead Group Zapier action. This action allows you to add leads to an active campaign."
---

## Step 1

Click on the **"Action"** step in the Zapier workflow and type in **"Teli AI"** in the search bar.

![Screenshot 2025-11-11 161813.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-11161813.png)

## Step 2

Click **"Account"** and sign in to your Teli AI account.

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 115814.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12115814.PNG)

## Step 3

Click the **"Trigger event"** menu options and select **"Upload Lead to Lead Group".**

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 122258.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12122258.PNG)

## Step 4

Click on the **"Configure"** tab. Click on the **"Lead Group"** menu options. This displays all your available lead groups. Minimum requirements for this action is the lead group and phone number.

(Note: If the list is empty, make sure you have a lead group present in your Teli account)

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 122546.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12122546.PNG)

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 122845.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12122845.PNG)

## Step 5

Once you've finished building yor zap and published it, whenever leads pass through the integration they should populate in the lead group you've selected.

(Note: It's highly advised that you start with a test lead to ensure the lead is populating in the correct lead group.)

![Screenshot 2025-11-12 122955.PNG](/images/Screenshot2025-11-12122955.PNG)
