<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card
    title="What Are Dynamic Variables?"
    icon="sliders"
    description="Dynamic Variables allow you to insert real-time data into your messages, providing a way to personalize and automate campaigns. This helps to engage with your audience in a more meaningful way by using their specific data."
  >
    **Personalized Messaging:** Add placeholders like `{customer_name}` to automatically populate messages with user-specific data.

    **Real-time Updates:** Information like `{first_name}` or `{location}` are replaced when the campaign is triggered, ensuring up-to-date communication.

    **Data-Driven Automation:** Simplify your processes by allowing dynamic content to adapt automatically based on user data.

  </Card>

  <Card
  title="How to Use Dynamic Variables"
  icon="pencil"
  description="To implement dynamic variables, include placeholders within your campaign's messages. When the campaign is triggered, these placeholders are replaced with the user’s actual data."
>
  **Step 1:** In the message body, use placeholders like `{first_name}`, `{company_name}`, or `{phone_number}`.

**Step 2:** Ensure the placeholders align with available user data, such as CRM fields.

**Step 3:** During execution, Teli AI replaces these placeholders with the real values from your database.

</Card>

  <Card
    title="Best Practices"
    icon="user"
    description="Follow these best practices to ensure seamless integration of dynamic variables into your campaigns."
  >
    
    **Clear Placeholders:** Make sure placeholders are descriptive and simple to avoid confusion (e.g., `{customer_name}` instead of `{user_info}`).

    **Test Thoroughly:** Run test campaigns with sample data to ensure the variables are correctly replaced before launching live campaigns.

  </Card>

  <Card
  title="Examples of Dynamic Variables"
  icon="flag"
  description="Here are various dynamic variables used to personalize communication."
>
  **Personal Information**
  `{{first_name}}` – The lead’s first name.
  `{{middle_name}}` – The lead’s middle name.
  `{{last_name}}` – The lead’s last name.
  `{{name}}` – The lead’s full name.
  `{{birthday}}` – The lead’s date of birth, formatted as MM/DD/YY.
  `{{email}}` – The lead’s email address.
  `{{phone_number}}` – The lead’s phone number, formatted as +1[Phone Number].

**Address Details**  
 `{{address_line_1}}` – The first line of the lead’s residential address.  
 `{{address_line_2}}` – The second line of the lead’s residential address (if applicable).  
 `{{city}}` – The city where the lead resides.  
 `{{state}}` – The state where the lead resides.  
 `{{zip_code}}` – The zip code of the lead’s residential address.  
 `{{country}}` – The country where the lead resides.

**Employment & Company Information**  
 `{{company_name}}` – The name of the lead’s employer or business.  
 `{{company_position}}` – The lead’s job title at the company.  
 `{{company_industry}}` – The industry in which the lead’s company operates.  
 `{{years_worked}}` – The number of years the lead has worked at their current job.  
 `{{company_address_line_1}}` – The first line of the company’s address.  
 `{{company_address_line_2}}` – The second line of the company’s address (if applicable).  
 `{{company_city}}` – The city where the company is located.  
 `{{company_state}}` – The state where the company is located.  
 `{{company_zip_code}}` – The zip code of the company’s address.  
 `{{company_country}}` – The country where the company is based.

**Financial Information**  
 `{{FICO_score}}` – The lead’s FICO credit score.  
 `{{loan_balance}}` – The total outstanding balance on the lead’s loan(s).  
 `{{loan_monthly_payment}}` – The monthly payment amount for the lead’s loan(s).  
 `{{loan_type}}` – The type of loan the lead has (e.g., mortgage, personal loan, auto loan).  
 `{{lender_name}}` – The name of the lender providing the loan.  
 `{{cc_balance}}` – The total credit card debt the lead has.  
 `{{savings}}` – The total savings the lead has.  
 `{{tax}}` – The amount of taxes the lead owes or has paid.  
 `{{mortgage_date}}` – The date when the lead’s mortgage was opened.  
 `{{months_since_mtg_open}}` – The number of months since the lead’s mortgage account was opened.

**Lead Tracking & Source Information**
`{{lead_source}}` – The source from which the lead was acquired (e.g., referral, advertisement, organic search).
`{{contact_id}}` – A unique identifier assigned to the lead.
`{{sso}}` – The lead’s social security number.

**Additional Notes**
`{{notes}}` – Any extra notes or comments related to the lead.

</Card>
