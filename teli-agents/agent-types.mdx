      <li><strong>Human-like conversations:</strong> Engages in real-time, natural speech interactions.</li>

      <li><strong>CRM & database integration:</strong> Pulls and updates customer data on the fly.</li>
      <li><strong>Personalized experiences:</strong> Customizes responses based on previous interactions.</li>
      <li><strong>24/7 availability:</strong> Ensures continuous customer engagement without downtime.</li>
    </ul>
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="SMS Agents"
    icon="message"
  >
    <ul>
      <li><strong>AI-driven two-way SMS:</strong> Holds real conversations via text.</li>
      <li><strong>Bulk messaging at scale:</strong> Sends thousands of personalized messages efficiently.</li>
      <li><strong>Human-in-the-loop escalation:</strong> Transfers complex queries to human agents when needed.</li>
      <li><strong>Smart message personalization:</strong> Adjusts tone and content dynamically.</li>
      <li><strong>Automated scheduling & reminders:</strong> Handles appointment confirmations and follow-ups.</li>
      <li><strong>Regulatory compliance:</strong> Ensures adherence to SMS guidelines and opt-in policies.</li>
    </ul>
