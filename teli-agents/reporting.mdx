# Reporting in Teli AI

Teli AI provides detailed reporting features that help you track the performance of your campaigns and make data-driven decisions. By using the reporting tools, you can gain valuable insights into user engagement, conversion rates, and more.

## Why Use Reporting?

Reporting allows you to:

- **Measure Campaign Performance**: Track the success of your campaigns and identify areas for improvement.
- **Make Data-Driven Decisions**: Use insights from reports to optimize your campaigns and engagement strategies.
- **Understand Audience Behavior**: Learn how your audience interacts with your content and campaigns.

## Popular Reporting Features

### 1. **Campaign Performance Reports**

Get detailed reports on the performance of your campaigns, including metrics such as:

- **Open Rates**: Track how many people opened your emails or messages.
- **Click-Through Rates**: Measure how many people clicked on links in your communications.
- **Conversion Rates**: See how many people completed a desired action, such as a purchase or sign-up.

### 2. **Audience Insights**

Understand how your audience is interacting with your campaigns. Get data on:

- **Demographics**: See age, location, and other demographic data about your audience.
- **Engagement Trends**: Track how audience engagement changes over time.
- **Behavioral Insights**: Understand what content resonates most with your audience.

### 3. **Exporting Reports**

Easily export your reports to analyze offline or share with your team. Reports can be exported in formats such as:

- **CSV**: Export data to CSV for further analysis.
- **PDF**: Download a report as a PDF to present to stakeholders.

### 4. **Customizable Dashboards**

Create custom dashboards that showcase the metrics and KPIs that matter most to you. Tailor your dashboard to focus on the data points that are most important for your business.

- **Drag-and-Drop Widgets**: Customize your dashboard by dragging and dropping various widgets.
- **Real-Time Data**: Get real-time updates on your campaigns' performance.

## Getting Started

To start using the reporting features, follow these steps:

1. **Navigate to the Reports Section**: Go to the Reports section in your Teli AI dashboard.
2. **Select a Campaign**: Choose a campaign to generate a performance report.
3. **Customize Your Report**: Use filters and widgets to focus on the metrics that matter most.
4. **Export or Share**: Export the report to CSV or PDF for further analysis or sharing.
