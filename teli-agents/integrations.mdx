# Integrating Teli AI with Other Platforms

Teli AI offers seamless integration capabilities that allow you to connect with various third-party platforms, expanding the scope and functionality of your AI-powered campaigns.

## Why Integrate with Teli AI?

Integrating Teli AI with your existing platforms can provide numerous benefits, including:

- **Improved Automation**: Automate customer interactions across various touchpoints such as voice, SMS, and email.
- **Data Syncing**: Ensure that customer data is consistent across platforms, improving overall user experience.
- **Scalability**: Easily scale your AI-powered campaigns to handle large volumes of interactions.

## Popular Integrations

### 1. **CRM Integrations**

Teli AI integrates with popular CRM systems to sync customer data and personalize interactions based on past behaviors and preferences.

- **Salesforce**
- **HubSpot**
- **Zoho CRM**

### 2. **Communication Platform Integrations**

Integrate with communication platforms to power voice, SMS, and email campaigns.

- **Twilio (SMS)**
- **Zoom (Voice)**
- **SendGrid (Email)**

### 3. **Payment Gateway Integrations**

Enable secure and automated payment processing directly within your campaigns.

- **Stripe**
- **PayPal**

### 4. **Social Media Integrations**

Teli AI also integrates with social media platforms for advanced AI-driven engagement.

- **Facebook Messenger**
- **WhatsApp**

## Getting Started

To get started with integrating Teli AI into your platform, follow these steps:

1. **Choose the integration** you want to set up from the list above.
2. **Follow the API documentation** for the platform you are integrating with to configure your API keys.
3. **Test the integration** to ensure everything is working as expected.
4. **Start using Teli AI** to engage with customers across various channels!
