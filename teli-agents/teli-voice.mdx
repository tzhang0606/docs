# Teli Voice: Powering Your Voice Campaigns

Teli AI enables you to create and manage voice campaigns, allowing you to automate calls, collect data, and engage with your customers more effectively. With Teli Voice, you can enhance your customer interactions and improve your campaign outcomes.

## Why Use Teli Voice?

Teli Voice offers several benefits for your business:

- **Automated Voice Calls**: Automate outbound calls for customer service, sales, and engagement.
- **Personalized Interactions**: Deliver personalized messages based on customer behavior and data.
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Get real-time analytics on call performance to optimize your campaigns.

## Key Features of Teli Voice

### 1. **Automated Outbound Calls**

Teli Voice allows you to set up automated calls to reach your customers with personalized messages.

- **Voice Campaigns**: Schedule and run automated voice campaigns for promotions, reminders, and updates.
- **Custom Scripts**: Tailor the script of each voice message to suit your brand and audience.
- **Interactive Voice Response (IVR)**: Set up automated menus to route customers based on their choices.

### 2. **Call Analytics**

Track and analyze your voice campaigns to measure their effectiveness and make adjustments.

- **Call Duration**: Measure how long your calls last and assess customer engagement.
- **Call Outcomes**: Track whether a call was answered, dropped, or went to voicemail.
- **Interactive Metrics**: Monitor the effectiveness of IVR menus and collect data on customer selections.

### 3. **SMS-to-Voice Integration**

Integrate SMS with voice campaigns to provide a seamless experience for your customers.

- **SMS Reminders**: Send SMS reminders with a call-to-action to schedule a voice call.
- **Cross-Channel Messaging**: Combine SMS and voice for multi-channel outreach.

### 4. **Campaign Scheduling**

Easily schedule when your voice campaigns will run. You can set up campaigns to run at:

- **Specific Times**: Schedule calls to go out at a time most convenient for your audience.
- **Recurring Intervals**: Set up recurring campaigns to reach customers on a regular basis.

## Getting Started with Teli Voice

To start using Teli Voice, follow these simple steps:

1. **Create a Voice Campaign**: Go to the Teli Voice dashboard and create a new voice campaign.
2. **Customize the Script**: Write the script for the automated voice message and add any IVR options.
3. **Set the Schedule**: Choose when the campaign should run, whether immediately or at a later time.
4. **Track Results**: After the campaign runs, review the analytics to assess performance and make improvements.
