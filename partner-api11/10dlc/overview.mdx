# 10DLC Registration Overview

10DLC (10 Digit Long Code) is required for business SMS messaging in the United States. This multi-step process verifies your business and messaging use case with carriers.

## Complete Registration Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create SubGroup" icon="folder">
    **Required.** Create an organizational subgroup for your brands.
    
    Time: Instant
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Register Brand" icon="building">
    Register your business with tax ID and contact details.
    
    Time: Instant (mock) or 1-2 hours (production)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Qualify Brand" icon="check">
    Verify your brand qualifies for your intended use case.
    
    Time: Instant
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Purchase Phone Number" icon="phone">
    Buy a dedicated 10DLC phone number.
    
    Time: Instant
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Campaign" icon="paper-plane">
    Submit campaign with sample messages and compliance details.
    
    Time: Instant submission, 1-2 days for carrier approval
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Carrier Approval" icon="hourglass">
    AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon review your campaign.
    
    Time: 1-2 business days (automatic)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Activation" icon="bolt">
    Webhook automatically activates your organization.
    
    Time: Instant upon approval
  </Step>
</Steps>
