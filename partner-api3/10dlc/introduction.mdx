# 10DLC Registration Overview

10DLC (10 Digit Long Code) is required for sending business SMS in the United States. This registration process verifies your business and use case with carriers.

<Warning>
Registration typically takes 1-2 business days for carrier approval.
</Warning>

## Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create SubGroup">
    **Required.** Create a subgroup to organize your brands and campaigns
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Register Your Business">
    Submit business details and tax information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Qualify Brand">
    Verify your business qualifies for your use case
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Campaign">
    Submit campaign details with sample messages
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Carrier Approval">
    Wait 1-2 days for AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon approval
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Phone Number Assignment">
    Receive your dedicated SMS phone number
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Use Cases

| Use Case | Description | Approval Time |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| `CUSTOMER_CARE` | Customer support | 1-2 days |
| `2FA` | Two-factor authentication | Instant |
| `ACCOUNT_NOTIFICATION` | Account updates | 1-2 days |
| `MARKETING` | Promotional messages | 3-5 days |

## Compliance Requirements

All campaigns must include:
- ✅ Clear opt-in process
- ✅ STOP keyword support
- ✅ HELP keyword support
- ✅ Privacy policy link
- ✅ Terms & conditions link
- ✅ Sample messages (3-5 examples)
